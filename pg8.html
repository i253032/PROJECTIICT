Our Story

Founded in 2018, Novu began with a simple yet ambitious vision: to reimagine the dining experience by blending local heritage with modern culinary techniques. What started as a small family-run kitchen has blossomed into a sanctuary for food lovers. At Novu, we believe that every meal tells a story, and we have dedicated ourselves to sourcing the finest organic ingredients to ensure that our story is one of quality, passion, and flavor.

Our Mission

To craft unforgettable moments through exceptional food and heartfelt hospitality. We strive to create a space where tradition meets innovation, ensuring every guest leaves feeling nourished, inspired, and at home.
