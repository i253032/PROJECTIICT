Our Story

Founded in 2018, Novu began with a simple yet ambitious vision: to reimagine the dining experience by blending local heritage with modern culinary techniques. What started as a small family-run kitchen has blossomed into a sanctuary for food lovers. At Novu, we believe that every meal tells a story.

Our Mission

To craft unforgettable moments through exceptional food and heartfelt hospitality. We strive to create a space where tradition meets innovation, ensuring every guest leaves feeling nourished, inspired, and at home.
